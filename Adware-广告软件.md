### Adware
#### Adware is software that displays advertisements on your computer.  
Adware displays advertising banners or pop-ups on your computer when you use an application. This is not necessarily a bad thing. 
Such advertising can fund the development of useful software, which is then distributed free (for example, 
Android apps and browser toolbars, many of which are adware funded).  

Adware becomes a problem if it:
* installs itself on your computer without your consent
* installs itself in applications other than the one it came with and displays advertising when you use those applications
* hijacks your web browser in order to display more ads
* gathers data on your web browsing without your consent and sends it to others via the Internet
* is designed to be difficult to uninstall  

Adware can slow down your PC. It can also slow down your Internet connection by downloading advertisements. 
Sometimes programming flaws in the adware can make your computer unstable.  

Some antivirus programs detect adware and report it as potentially unwanted applications.
You can then either authorize the adware program or remove it from your computer. There are also dedicated programs for detecting adware.

### 广告软件是在你的电脑上显示广告的软件  
当你使用应用时，广告软件在你的电脑上显示广告横幅和弹出窗，这并完全是个坏事，这样的广告可以资助开发有用的软件，并且免费分发（如Android应用和浏览器工具栏，
很多是广告软件资助的）    

当一个广告软件存在以下行为时，就成了问题：
* 未经你的同意就安装
* 安装在除自身以外的程序中，当你使用它们时，会显示广告
* 劫持你的网络浏览器，以显示更多的广告
* 未经你的允许收集浏览器数据并通过网络发送出去
* 难以卸载

广告软件可能会减慢你的电脑。 它也可以通过下载广告来降低你的网速。 有时在广告软件中的漏洞可能会使你的计算机不稳定。

一些反病毒软件会检测到广告软件并且报告为不需要安装的应用。
你可以授权给广告软件或者删除它们。有些程序可以专门用来检测广告软件。
